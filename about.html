About Us

Learn about Rashmi, the founder of Isha Home Staging

Rashmi is a CSP- Certified Staging Professional. Rashmi was personally trained by 5-times Emmy Award winning, HGTV star Television Celebrity and seasoned Interior Designer Cathy Hobbs

Rashmi seamlessly bridges the traditional with the contemporary in her decorating aesthetics. No detail is too small or too great for Her, for when she has a concept or a mood in mind for a space, everything from floor to the ceiling to the lighting, to the last cushion will be chosen with intent and an expert's eye. Her style is eclectic and bold, yet restrained.

Every individual space could use its own unique look reflecting a certain personality, taste and sensibility. Our first priority is to make you feel house proud.. to create a warm cozy abode you and your buyers want to just keep coming back to. We spend a lot of time in our homes which are an extended personality of ourselves- making memories, entertaining and rejuvenating. So our home needs to be something we are not only proud of but happen to love very much.

Isha home staging is a unique place that caters to people who are tired of shopping at generic stores that look and feel the same. Isha Home staging also caters to the sellers' needs. At Isha our priority is to stage a warm cozy inviting home that buyers fall in love with instantly.
